Science Trivia for kids

Physical and Non-Physical Science Trivia

  1. How many senses do humans have?

    We have more than five senses. Neurological studies have found that we have nine or more senses

  2. What is the heart used for?

    The heart is used to pump blood all over the body

  3. How many bones do humans have?

    We have 270 bones at birth but at adulthood some bones fuse together that sum up to 206 bones

  4. What is the process of plants taking food from the soil through the sun?

    Photosynthensis

  5. What are the openings on a plants surface?

    The Stomata

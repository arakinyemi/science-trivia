Atom Science Trivia

  1. What do atoms make up of?

    Everything

  2. How many parts do atoms have?

    We have three parts of an atom

  3. What are the parts of an atom?

    The neutron, the proton and the electron

  4. What are atoms like in solids?

    They are packed together

  5. What are atoms like in liquid?

    They flow freely
